On Foot

Day Hiking Trails

The Chequamegon-Nicolet National Forest surrounds the Three Lakes area, offering extensive trail networks managed by the US Forest Service. Better printed maps for some trails are available at the Three Lakes Information Bureau.

Hiking

Hidden Lakes Trail

Rated the #1 trail in the AllTrails listing for Nicolet National Forest. A classic north woods hike through forest and around lakes.

Nicolet North Trail

Paradise for mountain biking and cross-country skiing, and a great multi-use trail network in the area. The Hidden Lakes Trail passes through this corridor.
