Access Requirements

How to gain access

To view the resident directory you must be a member of the "lakejuliapeople" Google Group and be logged into Google with the same email address registered in the group.

Not yet a member? Learn how to join the email list →

Already a member but having trouble? The group may have a different email address on file. Contact the administrator to sort it out.

📋

Directory is maintained separately from the email list

The resident directory and the "lakejuliapeople" mailing list are independent records. Being on one does not automatically mean you are on the other. The directory is a broader list of property owners; the email list includes all regular lake users.
