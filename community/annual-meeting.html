About the Meeting

Our annual gathering

The Annual Meeting of Members is the central event of the Association year. It is held on a weekend day shortly before or after the Fourth of July — timed to maximize participation while as many families as possible are at the lake.

The meeting covers required governance business: election of directors, financial reports, and any other matters before the membership. In practice it doubles as a community picnic, with neighbors from all corners of the lake coming together.

The Board of Directors also holds its required annual meeting on the same day, typically immediately before or after the Members' meeting.
