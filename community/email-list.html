Overview

lakejuliapeople: our community list

The Lake Julia Association uses a Google Group email list called "lakejuliapeople" to stay connected with lake residents and regular users, currently around 200 people. The group serves two purposes: it's how we communicate as a community, and it's the access mechanism that protects private content on this site (like the resident directory) from the general public. Rather than building a separate login system, we use Google Group membership as a lightweight privacy layer; only verified members can view restricted materials.

Messaging has been infrequent and the list has never been abused. Membership is open to anyone who regularly uses Lake Julia property, including family members, grown children of owners, and friends who visit regularly. If you know someone who should be included, encourage them to reach out.

⚠️

Think before you send

Messages sent to lakejuliapeople@googlegroups.com go to approximately 200 people. Use this address thoughtfully, and be careful with "Reply All" when responding to list messages.

Getting Access

How to join the list

Joining is straightforward: just four steps, and most people are already halfway there:

  1. Choose the email address you'd like to use. This is where list messages will be delivered, and it's how the site will recognize you as a member, so pick one you check regularly.
  2. Make sure that email address is connected to a Google account. A Google account is simply a login at google.com; it has nothing to do with Android or Gmail. If you've ever used YouTube, Google Maps, or Google Drive (even on an iPhone), you already have one. To check, visit accounts.google.com and try signing in with your chosen email. If you don't have one, you can create a free Google account using any email address (no Gmail required).
  3. Email the site administrator and ask to be added to the "lakejuliapeople" Google Group. Just include the email address from step 1 and we'll take it from there.
  4. Once you've been added, list messages will start arriving in your inbox. To view restricted content on this site, open your browser, go to accounts.google.com, and sign in with that same email, and you're all set.
💡

Not sure if you have a Google account?

Go to accounts.google.com and try signing in with your email. If it works, you're already set for step 2. If not, creating one is free and takes about two minutes.

Restricted Materials

What requires group membership

Certain documents, directories, and images on this site are restricted to group members. To access them you must be both:

  1. Subscribed to the "lakejuliapeople" Google Group, and
  2. Logged into a Google account using the same email address as your group subscription.

If you believe you should have access but can't view restricted materials, contact the administrator; the group may have a different email address on file for you.
