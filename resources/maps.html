House Map

Lake Julia Neighbors

49 identified properties numbered #1–49 beginning at the public boat landing, color-coded by access road. Updated episodically since 2002 and sized at 18" × 24" — prints clearly at full size. House numbers are used throughout this site to identify properties and families.

Depth Map

Bathymetric Map

Wisconsin DNR depth survey of the lake bed (1975), plus a simplified version highlighting the more treacherous rock beds and shallow areas. Observations through 2020 show no significant changes from the original survey.

GIS

LJA Interactive Map

An interactive map of Lake Julia and its surrounding landscape. Explore all 49 properties, access roads, hydrology, land parcels, and terrain. Search residents by name, click any feature for details, or use the drawing tools to sketch notes and measurements. Opening in fullscreen opens a new tab in your browswer and is highly recommended.

Wisconsin DNR Surface Water Data Viewer

The WI DNR tool used to identify water resources, wetlands, floodplains, and dams. It allows users to view, analyze, and download data on water quality, fisheries, and construction permits. Opening in fullscreen opens a new tab in your browswer and is highly recommended.

County Parcel Maps
